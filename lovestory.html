Our Story

2020

First Meeting

Our first meeting was in 2020 when I (John) was in grade 11 and she (Tenu) was in grade 6, having completed grades 10 and 5, respectively. At that time, I was staying at my uncle's house during a break while the COVID-19 pandemic was ongoing.

2020

The First Letter

Initially, Tenu reached out to me by writing a letter, referencing the love of Jesus and Peter from the Bible. I was surprised and confused, questioning what kind of friendship she wanted. I tried to avoid answering her, but she insisted.

2020

My Response

My response was another written letter stating, "It is God's will for us to meet together this way." However, she sought a more definitive answer that reflected my acceptance of being her friend (or boyfriend).

2020

Distance

I decided to distance myself from her and told her, "It is not the right time," when she approached me in person. This made her feel sad; she cried and felt hurt, but she still loved me. Despite my distance, she didn't lose hope and continued to reach out to me via phone calls and text messages. I loved her too but felt it was not the right time for us to be together.

2024

God's Perfect Timing

Eventually, God brought me back to her, and we started dating in 2024. I realized it was indeed God's will for us to be together.

2024

The Challenge

A few months later, she sent me a message saying, "We have to break up," which left me frustrated and downcast. The reason for her message was that her family did not want us to be together.

I felt anxious and frustrated; I tried calling her many times, but there was no answer. Finally, on a Sunday morning, she answered my call, and I felt a surge of happiness.

2024

Renewed Commitment

The day before New Year's Eve, I had a dream that strengthened our relationship and helped us bond even more. We began our dating journey anew, and I gave her a watch as a gift through Yonas, my uncle's son, which symbolized that it was the right time for us to love each other.

Now

Future Together

As we look ahead, we dream of a life built on the foundation of Christ. We envision a marriage that honors God, a home filled with love and prayer,stable family and a testimony that encourages others. We're excited to see how God will continue to write our story, knowing that His plans for us are good (Jeremiah 29:11). Our prayer is that through every season of life, we'll grow closer to Him and to each other.

Thank God

For Bringing Us Together

We thank God for orchestrating our meeting and guiding our relationship according to His will.

For Teaching Us Love

We're grateful for learning Christ-like love through our relationship.

For Overcoming Challenges

Thank God for giving us strength during difficult times and bringing us through them.

For Our Future

We praise God for the marriage He is preparing us for and the life we'll build together in Him.
